Frequently Asked Questions
Can I switch plans at any time?▾
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you pay the prorated difference. When downgrading, the new rate applies at the next billing cycle.
What payment methods do you accept?▾
We accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express via Stripe, local payments via PayFast (South Africa) and Paystack (Africa), cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH, USDT, SOL), and IBAN bank transfers for EU customers.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?▾
The Free tier gives you permanent access to public features. Paid plans include a 14-day money-back guarantee so you can try risk-free.
What happens if I exceed my API limits?▾
Requests beyond your daily limit are queued and throttled rather than blocked. You will receive a notification to upgrade. Enterprise and Platform tiers have burst capacity built in.
Do you offer annual discounts?▾
Yes. Annual billing saves 20% on all paid plans. Contact sales for custom multi-year agreements on Enterprise and Platform tiers.
How does the white-label option work?▾
Platform tier customers get a fully branded deployment of Bridge AI OS under their own domain with custom theming, dedicated infrastructure, and their own billing system for end users.

Need a custom solution?

Enterprise and Platform customers get dedicated onboarding, custom SLAs, and priority engineering support.
