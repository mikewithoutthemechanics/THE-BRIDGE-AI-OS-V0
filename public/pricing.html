Frequently Asked Questions
Can I switch plans at any time?▾
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you pay the prorated difference. When downgrading, the new rate applies at the next billing cycle.
What payment methods do you accept?▾
We accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express via PayFast (South Africa). EFT and additional methods coming soon.
What happens if I exceed my free tier limits?▾
You will see a notification suggesting an upgrade to Pro. Your existing work is never lost. Upgrade anytime to continue with unlimited AI tasks.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?▾
The Free tier gives you permanent access to basic features. Paid plans include a 14-day money-back guarantee so you can try risk-free.
