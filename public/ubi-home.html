How UBI Works

A self-sustaining income floor funded by AI-generated revenue.

💸

Revenue Allocation

30% of all Bridge AI OS revenue is automatically deposited into the UBI pool via smart contract. No manual intervention required.

📅

Monthly Distribution

Funds are distributed equally to all verified beneficiaries on the 1st of each month via mobile money or crypto wallet.

👥

Beneficiary Verification

KYC-verified enrollment with biometric identity. Sybil-resistant registration ensures fair distribution to real people.

🔍

Full Transparency

Every transaction is on-chain and auditable. Real-time dashboards show pool inflows, outflows, and per-capita calculations.
